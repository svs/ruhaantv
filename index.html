What we do

As the parent of a small child, I found myself becoming increasingly dissatisfied with the available entertainment options for children.

TV has to cater to a very wide audience and so wasn't always suitable for someone Ruhaan's age. Also, I'm not a fan of the incessant advertising and the hyperexcitement on the screens. Also, what about the classics like Dr. Seuss and the old Disney films? Where to find those on TV?

Standard youtube left a lot to be desired. Ruhaan would be watching cartoons one moment and then in two clicks be watching Portugese motocross.

To solve these problems, I began to curate video content for Ruhaan to watch and wow - there's so much nice stuff out there. Every week, I put what I think he will like in a video playlist for him and then I see which videos he watches over and over.

Every week, I now share Ruhaan's favourite videos. You can also receive handpicked video playlists guaranteed to keep your child engaged, smiling and consuming perfectly age-appropriate material and to give you, poor parent, an hour of peace every day :-)

Just tell me your email address and I'll add you to the mailing list. The service is free until March 31 2013 after which it will be Rs. 300 per month for four 1-hour playlists.
